The Origin

일할 맛 나는 사무실
An office worth working in

December 24, 2018. While Korea celebrated Christmas Eve, Kim Heon filed incorporation papers for a company called WEFUN. Capital: ₩50 million — roughly $45,000. Address: a basement office in Gasan Digital Complex, Seoul's startup district.

The co-founders brought 10+ previous startup experiences between them. Serial entrepreneurs who had seen failure enough times to recognize a genuine opportunity. The insight was deceptively simple: Korean offices were broken, and nobody was fixing them holistically.

They started with Snack24 — subscription snack delivery for offices. One box at a time. But the ambition was always bigger: to build the operating system for every workplace in Korea, and eventually Asia.

The Platform

~100 modular services
across four pillars

What started as snack delivery now spans the entire workplace experience. WEFUN operates roughly 100 modular services organized into four strategic pillars:

🍎
Employee Welfare
Snack24, Coffee24, Lunch24, wellness products. The core that started it all — making daily office life better, one delivery at a time. Over 10,000 B2B clients and 800,000 daily touchpoints.
🎁
Corporate Gifting SaaS
Gift24 platform. Korea's gifting culture (설날, 추석, company milestones) is massive. WEFUN digitized the entire process — catalog, personalization, delivery, reporting.
🏢
Office Operations
Office supplies, cleaning, maintenance, safety equipment. The overlap zone with Lyreco — the procurement decision-maker (총무팀) is identical for both companies' products.
📐
Space Management
Office design, renovation, digital signage (1,000+ screens). Acquired Pudding for workspace management. Making physical offices work better, not just the supplies in them.

The Narrative

Stories to Tell in the Room

These are the narratives that will resonate with a French family business evaluating a young Korean company. Each one is designed to build trust, demonstrate character, and create emotional connection.

The Christmas Eve Founding

"12월 24일 — 모두가 쉴 때 시작했습니다"

WEFUN was incorporated on Christmas Eve 2018. While others rested, Kim Heon was filing papers. This story signals relentless drive — the kind Bigeard will recognize, because he joined Lyreco in his twenties and never stopped pushing.

Basement to Tower

"가산디지털 지하실에서 강남 15층까지"

Started at Gasan Digital Complex basement with ₩50M → now at K-Square Gangnam 15F, 252 employees, ₩89.2B revenue. This mirrors Lyreco's own journey from a bookshop to a €2.2B empire. The parallel is powerful.

Speed of Profitability

"창업 수개월 만에 월 흑자"

Monthly P&L breakeven in 2019, just months after founding. This isn't a cash-burning tech startup hoping for eventual profitability. WEFUN makes money. This will comfort finance-minded Helen Riley.

The Retention Story

"97% — 한 번 들어오면 떠나지 않습니다"

97% client retention. Once WEFUN enters an office, they almost never leave. For Lyreco — a company built on long-term client relationships — this statistic speaks their language perfectly.

The M&A Philosophy

"인수는 통합이 아니라 성장입니다"

Six acquisitions, each preserving the acquired team's identity while plugging into the WEFUN platform. The Human Food deal — PE-partnered with Epic Platform + Hana S&B — showed WEFUN can structure sophisticated transactions. Tell this story to prove Lyreco's Asian team will be respected, not absorbed.

800,000 Daily Lives

"매일 80만 명의 직장인 삶에 닿습니다"

WEFUN touches 800,000 office workers daily across Korea. This isn't a niche SaaS — it's infrastructure for how Korea works. Scale this across Lyreco's 5 Asian markets and the impact multiplies dramatically.
