Strategic Context

Asia in Perspective

Lyreco's Asian operations represent approximately 3% of group revenue — a fraction of the company's €3 billion European core. This is not a criticism; it's a reflection of strategic priority. Europe has been — and should remain — the heartland.

But 3% also represents untapped potential. Five established markets with infrastructure, client relationships, and regulatory compliance already in place. The question isn't whether these operations have value — it's whether they can reach their full potential from 9,000 kilometers away.

A locally-rooted operator, deeply embedded in Asian markets, with proven technology and logistics infrastructure, could unlock what distance and divided attention cannot.

리레코의 아시아 사업은 그룹 매출의 약 3%를 차지합니다 — 30억 유로 유럽 핵심 사업의 일부입니다. 이는 비판이 아닌 전략적 우선순위의 반영입니다.

하지만 3%는 또한 미개발 잠재력을 의미합니다. 인프라, 고객 관계, 규제 준수가 이미 갖춰진 5개 시장. 문제는 이 사업이 가치가 있느냐가 아니라, 9,000km 떨어진 곳에서 최대 잠재력에 도달할 수 있느냐입니다.

WeFun is not a startup with aspirations — it is a scaled platform with seven years of compounding growth. Three qualities matter most for this partnership:

Platform Technology: WeFun's integrated system connects suppliers, logistics, and client offices. This technology layer is precisely what Lyreco's Asian operations need to compete with local players.

Local Expertise: WeFun operates from Gangnam-gu — the same neighborhood as Lyreco Korea. They understand the Korean market at a granular level, from 총무팀 (general affairs team) purchasing patterns to last-mile delivery logistics.

M&A Integration Track Record: Six acquisitions, all successfully integrated. Acquired teams retained and empowered. This is not a company that absorbs and discards — it enhances and grows.

위펀은 포부만 있는 스타트업이 아닌 — 7년간 복리 성장을 이룬 확장된 플랫폼입니다. 플랫폼 기술, 현지 전문성, M&A 통합 실적 — 이 세 가지 자질이 파트너십에 가장 중요합니다.

Our Commitment

People First

Lyreco's approximately 700 Asian employees represent decades of institutional knowledge. Any transition must protect their careers, their expertise, and their dignity.

WeFun's track record provides concrete evidence:

  • 800 employees with strong retention rates
  • Unlimited PTO policy — rare in Korean corporate culture
  • Consistently high employee satisfaction scores
  • Six acquisitions where acquired teams were retained and given expanded roles

This is not a company that treats people as line items. WeFun's own culture is the strongest guarantee of how Lyreco's employees will be treated.

리레코의 약 700명 아시아 직원은 수십 년의 제도적 지식을 대표합니다. 모든 전환은 그들의 경력, 전문성, 존엄성을 보호해야 합니다. 위펀의 실적은 구체적인 증거를 제공합니다.

Convergence

Building on What Korea Started

Lyreco Korea's "Lounge Service" brand is remarkable — a B2B wellness platform offering food, snacks, office operations support, and corporate gifting. It's exactly the kind of service that WeFun has built its entire business around.

This overlap is not coincidence; it's convergence. Two organizations, operating in the same city, serving the same customer need, reached the same conclusion independently. WeFun doesn't replace Lounge Service — it accelerates it. Deeper technology, broader catalog, faster logistics.

리레코 코리아의 "라운지 서비스" 브랜드는 주목할 만합니다 — 음식, 간식, 사무실 운영 지원, 기업 선물을 제공하는 B2B 웰니스 플랫폼. 위펀이 전체 사업을 구축한 것과 정확히 같은 종류의 서비스입니다.

Vision

Total Workplace Platform

The combined entity creates something that doesn't exist in Asia today: a Total Workplace Platform spanning office supplies, workplace wellness, food and beverage, corporate gifting, and safety — all delivered through a unified technology platform with local expertise in every market.

For Lyreco, this means:

  • Asian operations reaching their full revenue potential
  • Brand legacy preserved and enhanced
  • Employees gaining access to a growing platform with expanded career opportunities
  • Continued connection to Lyreco's global network and values

통합 기업은 오늘날 아시아에 존재하지 않는 것을 만듭니다: 사무용품, 직장 웰니스, 식음료, 기업 선물, 안전을 아우르는 토탈 워크플레이스 플랫폼.
