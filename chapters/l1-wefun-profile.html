Origins

Christmas Eve 2018

Christmas Eve 2018. Kim Heon (김헌) incorporated WeFun on December 24, 2018, with initial capital of €35K. While most founders start in January, Kim chose the most unlikely date — a signal of the urgency and conviction that would define the company.

WeFun began as a snack delivery service for offices — a simple idea that addressed a real gap in Korean workplace culture. From that basement in Gasan Digital Complex, a vision emerged: build the operating system for every workplace in Korea.

2018년 크리스마스 이브. 김헌 대표는 2018년 12월 24일 자본금 €35K로 위펀을 설립했습니다. 대부분의 창업자가 1월에 시작할 때, 김 대표는 가장 특별한 날을 택했습니다 — 이후 회사를 정의할 긴박감과 확신의 신호였습니다.

"While most founders start in January, Kim chose Christmas Eve — a signal of the urgency and conviction that would define everything that followed."

From €35K in seed capital to €138M in annual revenue in just seven years. That trajectory — an estimated 130% compound annual growth rate — places WeFun among the fastest-growing B2B platforms in Korean history.

The growth was not fueled by unsustainable marketing spend but by organic client expansion and strategic M&A. Monthly P&L breakeven was achieved in 2019, just months after founding. This is not a cash-burning startup — WeFun makes money.

자본금 €35K에서 연매출 €138M까지 단 7년. 이 궤적은 — 추정 130% 연평균 성장률 — 위펀을 한국 역사상 가장 빠르게 성장하는 B2B 플랫폼 중 하나로 자리매김합니다.

WeFun operates four core pillars: Snack24 (office snack subscription — the founding product), Coffee24 (premium coffee service for workplaces), Lunch24 (corporate meal solutions), and Gift24 (corporate gifting platform).

Beyond these pillars, WeFun has expanded to offer approximately 100 modular workplace services, creating a "Total Workplace Platform" approach. The platform technology connects suppliers, logistics, and client offices through a unified system — one invoice, one dashboard, everything the office needs.

Multiple funding rounds completed, culminating in a €14M Series C (July 2023) at a €76M post-money valuation. Lead investors include institutional funds that validated both the business model and the growth trajectory.

The Series C valuation demonstrates institutional investor confidence. More importantly, WeFun has the financial capacity to close an acquisition of Lyreco Asia's scale. With strong unit economics and improving margins, the company is positioned to absorb and integrate significant new operations without financial strain.

IPO trajectory: Hana Securities appointed as lead underwriter (February 2024). Preliminary KRX examination filing planned for April 2026. Estimated IPO market cap target: €138–207M.
